Project Overview

The dashboard analyzes an enterprise risk register and calculates risk scores based on impact and probability levels. Mitigation actions are tracked to support proactive risk management and decision making.

  • Risk scoring model (Impact × Probability)
  • Top risk identification
  • Mitigation action tracking
  • Interactive risk analytics
